Return & Refund Policy

Your satisfaction is important to us. Due to the perishable nature of our products, we have a specific policy for returns and refunds.

How to Return an Item

Eligibility for Returns: You can return your purchase within 2 days from the date of purchase. Please ensure the item is unused, in its original condition, and includes all tags and labels.


Starting the Return Process: To begin, send us a quick email at info.swadgalli@gmail.com to request a Return Authorization number. This number is essential for processing your return.


Sending Back Your Item: Once you have your Return Authorization number, carefully pack the item in its original packaging. Ship it to:

Processing Refunds or Exchanges:After we receive and inspect your return, we’ll process your refund or exchange within 3 business days. Refunds will be credited to your original payment method within 5 to 7 business days. You’ll get an email confirmation as soon as it’s done.

Damaged or Defective Products

If your order arrives damaged or is defective, please contact us within **7 days** of delivery. To process your request, please provide your order number and a clear photo of the damaged item. We will promptly arrange for a replacement or a full refund, including any shipping costs.

General Returns

We do not accept returns for any reason other than damage or defect. We guarantee that all our products are fresh and of the highest quality when they are shipped.

Order Cancellations

If you need to cancel your order, please contact us as soon as possible. We can only cancel orders that have not yet been shipped. Once a product has been dispatched, the order cannot be canceled.
