Our Brand Story

SwadGaLLi
जहाँ नेपाली स्वाद कहानी बन्छ


(Authentic Taste. Handcrafted with Heart.)

In every Nepali home, there's a street, a galli, filled with memories. It's where aama fries thekua golden before festivals, where baje breaks khurma during tea-time stories, and where children wait for their share of khajhuri after school.

That's where SwadGaLLi was born, not in a factory, but in the heart of those moments.

At SwadGaLLi, we believe taste isn't just about ingredients, it's about sambandha (connection), parampara (tradition), and maya (love). Every piece of our Thekua, Khurma, and Khajhuri is handmade, using age-old recipes passed down through Nepali kitchens, crafted with the same patience and pride that once filled our festivals and family gatherings.

We're here to keep those flavors alive to bring the warmth of home to every corner, and remind us that true taste never fades — it travels.

Because SwadGaLLi isn't just a snack brand it's the taste of ghar, packed with heritage, honesty, and heart.

Our Mission

Our mission is to lovingly craft and share the authentic flavors of Nepal's cherished traditional snacks, bringing the warmth of our culinary heritage and simple joys to homes everywhere.
